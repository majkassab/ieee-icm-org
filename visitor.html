Qatar

Qatar is where cultural authenticity meets modernity; where the sand meets the sea; where people come together to experience unique offerings in culture, sports, business and family entertainment. Qatar holds thrills and novel experiences for all. It offers a window to the past – and a glimpse into the future, all under the umbrella of that famous Arabian hospitality.

Applying for a Qatar Visa

For attendees of ICM 2024, most nationalities can apply for a tourist visa or business visa online. Here’s a general overview of the process:

  • Check Visa Requirements: Visit the Visit Qatar https://visitqatar.com/ to confirm visa requirements for your nationality. Qatar offers a visa-on-arrival option for citizens of over 80 countries.
  • Apply Online: For those requiring a pre-approved visa, you can apply through the Qatar Visa Service:
  • Website: Qatar Visa Service https://visitqatar.com/intl-en/plan-your-trip/visas
  • Required Documents: Passport copy, photo, and any additional documents as specified.
  • Processing Time: Typically 3-5 business days, but it’s best to apply well in advance.
  • Contact Information for Visa Assistance: https://visitqatar.com/intl-en/contact-us
