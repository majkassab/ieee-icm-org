Final Paper Submission


Papers accepted for Oral Presentation will be submitted for inclusion in the IEEE Xplore Digital Library.

Authors of Accepted Papers need to submit their Final Manuscripts prior to the deadline of September 30, 2025, using the following steps:

  • Create your manuscript(s) using the IEEE two-column format, check the samples below:
    Caution: Style files and templates provided here have been created to ensure that margin requirement is adequately met and do not add footer nor header.
    MS Word and LaTeX file templates
  • Proofread and check the layout of the manuscript.
  • Check your manuscript for IEEEXplore compatibility using the following steps:
    IEEE-ICM 2025 has been registered for use of IEEE PDF eXpress™. IEEE PDF eXpress is a free service to IEEE conferences, allowing their authors to make IEEE Xplore-compatible PDFs (Conversion function) or to check PDFs that authors have made themselves for IEEEXplore compatibility (PDF Check function).
    • Go to IEEE PDF eXpress: https://ieee-pdf-express.org/
    • Create IEEE PDF eXpress account. The ICM 2025 Conference ID: *******
    • Upload source file(s) (your manuscripts) for conversion if it is not in PDF format and/or upload your PDF file for checking if already in PDF format
    • Use IEEE PDF eXpress to attain IEEE Xplore-compatible PDF(s). The site contains extensive instructions, resources, helpful hints, and access to technical support.
  • Submit final, IEEE Xplore-compatible PDF(s) to ICM 2025 at https://edas.info/******

Please note: Uploading a paper to IEEE PDF Express is not the same as submitting the paper to the conference. You will still need to submit the checked PDF by the normal means through the EDAS website.
