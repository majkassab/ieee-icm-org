PROGRAM AT A GLANCE


Program
at a Glance

01

Keynote speeches and plenary talks by experts in the field of circuits and systems.

02

Technical sessions and workshops covering a wide range of topics related to circuits and systems, including analog and digital circuits, communication circuits and systems, bio-inspired circuits and systems, power electronics, signal processing, and more.

03

Poster sessions, demonstrations, and exhibits showcasing the latest research and technology in circuits and systems.

04

Networking events, receptions, and social activities providing opportunities for attendees to connect and collaborate with colleagues, peers, and industry experts.

05

Student activities, including competitions, poster sessions, and career development sessions, aimed at fostering the next generation of circuits and systems professionals.

06

Special sessions and panels featuring discussions on emerging trends, challenges, and opportunities in the field of circuits and systems.

07

Awards ceremonies recognizing outstanding contributions and achievements in circuits and systems research and development.

08

Industry forums and tutorials aimed at bridging the gap between academia and industry, and providing insights into the latest trends and technologies in circuits and systems.
